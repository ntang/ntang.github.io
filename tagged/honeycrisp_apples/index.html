Bento in Under 5 Minutes

Organic tri-color seedless grapes; Honeycrisp apple slices; natural peanut butter (peanuts and sea salt —period) for dipping; 7 grain mini roll

The Little Ladies couldn’t decide between a peanut butter sandwich or apple slices with peanut butter for dipping this morning, so we went with the option for both by tucking a mini 7 grain roll into the box.  This bento, which was created in under 5 minutes, had approval ratings that were through the roof.

Note:  Almond butter is an excellent substitution for peanut butter.  My kids love both.

Introduction

Little Ladies Who Lunch was inspired by a diagnosis of FAMILIAL HYPERCHOLESTEROLEMIA for 3 out of 6 household members (myself and my 2 young daughters) just days before the start of the 2010-2011 school year.

The panic that ensued quickly gave way to "when life gives you lemons, make lemonade." And preserved lemons. And lemon roasted chicken. And low-fat lemon yogurt cake. You get the picture.
