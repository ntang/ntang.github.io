The Soft Serve Fruit Co.

I don’t normally utilize this blog to write about things I don’t make myself, but good golly maybe I should start.  And hey, companies that are actually trying to make a fantastic, healthier product should be applauded and recognized publicly, right?

I was coming home from our yearly pediatric appointments with the Little Ladies (they had just braved having 8 vials of blood drawn each) when we spied The Soft Serve Fruit Co.  “What is this place?” I wondered.

Here’s what we learned:

The Soft Serve Fruit Co. uses only 3 ingredients in their product:

  • seasonal fruit
  • filtered water
  • a touch of cane sugar

End of story. And the products are non-dairy, free of fat, sodium, cholesterol and gluten.  A small cup of Soft Serve Fruit (which is actually a very generous serving size) is less than 90 calories, and it tastes fantastic.  The 3 of us left feeling gleeful and refreshed (we each had a serving of the mango soft serve — out of this world).

Toppings are available, and they’re often organic and free of nitrates, coloring, preservatives, high fructose corn syrup, etc. 

I wish more companies like The Soft Serve Fruit Co. existed.  Luckily for my lactose-intolerant husband, there is a location only a few short blocks from where he works. 

There are currently 3 locations in New York City.  Click here for further information (including contact info so you can harass the owners about opening a location near you).  :0) 

Note:  This post was not sponsored or influenced in any way other than by my taste buds and my desire to support companies that care about the products they serve.
