Baked Chicken Katsu

Baked chicken katsu, brown rice, steamed edamame with sea salt; fruit salad of fresh pineapple and organic raspberries.

Yes, chicken for a second day (by request).  For April Kim’s baked chicken katsu recipe, click here.  Note:  we used whole wheat flour as a substitutions for all-purpose flour, and the Little Ladies sometimes love a squeeze of lemon on their katsu right before serving.

The brine makes the chicken butter-soft.  Genius!

Introduction

Little Ladies Who Lunch™ was inspired by a diagnosis of FAMILIAL HYPERCHOLESTEROLEMIA for 3 out of 6 household members (myself and my 2 young daughters) just days before the start of the 2010-2011 school year.

The panic that ensued quickly gave way to "when life gives you lemons, make lemonade." And preserved lemons. And lemon roasted chicken. And lemon yogurt cake. You get the picture.
