Happy St. Patrick's Day!
To make a shamrock apple, use a cookie cutter and push it slightly into a Golden Delicious apple.  Next use a knife to chisel out the shape of the cut.  Then, use the cookie cutter to cut out a chunk of Granny Smith apple.  Insert the Granny Smith shamrock into the golden delicious.  Done. Inspired by A Mum ‘N the Oven.
