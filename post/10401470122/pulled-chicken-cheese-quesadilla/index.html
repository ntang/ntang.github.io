Pulled Chicken & Cheese Quesadilla

Pulled free-range/antibiotic-free chicken & a mix of light cheddar and mozzarella cheeses on whole wheat grilled tortilla; red seedless grapes; Bosc pear slices

Today’s lunch is a great use of leftovers from a dinner of whole roasted chicken.  There was just enough meat left on the frame to assemble these quesadillas, but we didn’t stop there.   Right now the frame is simmering in a large pot loaded with soup veggies for stock.  The house smells terrific, and every bit of the chicken has been used!

Introduction

Little Ladies Who Lunch™ was inspired by a diagnosis of FAMILIAL HYPERCHOLESTEROLEMIA for 3 out of 6 household members (myself and my 2 young daughters) just days before the start of the 2010-2011 school year.

The panic that ensued quickly gave way to "when life gives you lemons, make lemonade." And preserved lemons. And lemon roasted chicken. And lemon yogurt cake. You get the picture.
