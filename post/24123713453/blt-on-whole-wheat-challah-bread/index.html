BLT on Whole Wheat Challah Bread

Humanely-raised, nitrate-free crispy turkey bacon, mixed spring lettuce, beef steak tomato and avocado slices (in lieu of mayo) on whole wheat challah bread; sweet corn; baby spinach with carrot and red raw pepper slices; plum balls and red seedless grapes

Don’t stop at lunch — we enthusiastically recommend BLTs as a quick and easy make-ahead summertime dinner.  Just cook whatever bacon you’ll be using in advance (we love all types of bacon — the Applegate Farms brand of turkey crisps up nicely when cooked in the oven, but we also like pork bacon and even duck bacon, all nitrate/antibiotic-free varieties, of course) and assemble the sandwich just in time for the meal.  Serve with healthy fruit and veggie sides. 

If your kids don’t fancy the idea of eating anything besides bacon on their sandwich, try a deconstructed version.  If you are a grain-free household, a BLT salad is also insanely delicious.
